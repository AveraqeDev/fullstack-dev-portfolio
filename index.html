About Me

My passion for code came when I was at the ripe young age of 15. I started by making mods for the popular game 'Minecraft.'

Since then my passion has grown beyond just making mods and has evolved into full-on web development. I love getting my 'brain dirty' with tricky problems and algorithms and creating fun applications. I get excited about working on big projects with teams. It is awesome to see the collaboration of like-minded people produce an amazing application!

When I am not coding you can find me:

  • Playing video games on one of my many consoles.
  • Binge watching everything on Disney+
